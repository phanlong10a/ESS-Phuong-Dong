ABOUT US

In 2011, East Star Shipping Co., Ltd. was founded and has grown up since then owing to the hard work and dedication by a group of young, motivated, educated and plentifylly experienced maritime professionals sharing common vision and purposes. So far, the company has found it pround to cater for many established and internationally recognized Principals, such as, Apex Ship Management Pte., Ltd, Midocean Marine Ltd., Alfa Ship Manager Pte., Ltd. and Union Marine Management Services Pte., Ltd from Singapore, Zeaborn Shipmanagement from Germany, Synergy Marine from India, and Bahri Ship Management from Arab Saudi. Currently, the company is having hundreds of seafarers at one time on board more than 40 vessels consisting of Handymax and Panamax bulk carriers, and container vessels of maximum 8,000- TEU capacity.

Though the company is still young, it has seen a strong and persistent growth for the past years because the company has constantly made itself more competitive, become more and more appealing for Vietnamese crew by providing better and better services to its crew. So far, the company has created a network of expansive connections among the Vietnamese crew community. Thus, it is always in a good position to source and recruit good Vietnamese crew to meet any order from its Principals. The company is projected to become a leading manning agent in Vietnam by 2025 when it will have had a pool of 1,500 seafarers with around 700 seafarers on board at one time. To achieve this ambition, the company will continue strengthening its capabilities by hiring additional good shore staff, expanding its own office premise, establishing a crew training center, and owning vessels to provide its seafarers with sea-based training.

VISION

East Star Shipping aims to develop into a leading shipping company in Vietnam, operating in the three core business scopes in a professional manner, namely, ship operation, crew management, and shipping agency

MISSION

East Star Shipping operates as a bridge to connect Vietnamese crew to and intergrate them into international shipping community through which they can improve themselves, and bring about a better life/brighter future for them and their families.
